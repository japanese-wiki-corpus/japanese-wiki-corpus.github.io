Shijo Station (四条駅)

Shijo stations are located in Kyoto City, Kyoto Prefecture. Two stations called Shijo Station exist, and they're located 1.1 km apart on an east-west axis.

Shijo Station (of the Keihan Electric Railway) - a station in Higashiyama Ward on the Keihan Main Line
The name is scheduled to be changed to Gion-shijo Station in fiscal year 2008.

Shijo Station (of the Kyoto Municipal Subway) - a station in Shimogyo Ward on the Karasuma Line of the Kyoto Municipal Subway
