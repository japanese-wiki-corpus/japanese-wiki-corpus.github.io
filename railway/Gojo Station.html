Gojo Station

Gojo Station (of the Keihan Electric Railway) - a station in Higashiyama Ward on the Keihan Main Line
The name is scheduled to be changed to Kiyomizu-gojo Station in fiscal year 2008.

Gojo Station (of Kyoto Municipal Subway) - a station in Shimogyo Ward of Kyoto City on the Kyoto Municipal Subway's Karasuma Line

Gojo Station (Nara Prefecture) - a station in Gojo City, Nara Prefecture on the Wakayama Line of the West Japan Railway Company (JR West)

Nishitetsu-Gojo Station - a station in Dazaifu City, Fukuoka Prefecture on the Nishitetsu Dazaifu Line
