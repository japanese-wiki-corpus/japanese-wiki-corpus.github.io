Matsue NAKAMURA (中村松江)

"Matsue NAKAMURA" is a name which has been used by several Kabuki (a traditional form of drama and music performed by male actors) actors.

Matsue NAKAMURA the First
He later became Riko NAKAMURA the First.

Matsue NAKAMURA the Second
He was a son of Hisazo ICHIKAWA.

Matsue NAKAMURA the Third
He later became Tomijuro NAKAMURA the Second.

Matsue NAKAMURA the Fourth
He was an adopted son of Utaemon NAKAMURA the Third.

Matsue NAKAMURA the Fifth
He later became Kaishun NAKAMURA the Second.

Matsue NAKAMURA the Sixth
He is the eldest son of Tozo NAKAMURA the Sixth. He succeeded to the name in April 2006 and changed his name from his previous stage name Tamataro NAKAMURA the Fourth to Matsue NAKAMURA.
