Fusateru HOSHO (宝生英照)

Fusateru HOSHO was a Noh actor of the Hosho school of shite-kata (lead actors). The 19th head of the Hosho school of shite-kata.

He was the eldest son of Fusao HOSHO, the 18th head of the Hosho school. In 1988, he succeeded to the head of the school. The holder of Important Intangible Cultural Property.

Kazufusa HOSHO is his eldest son.
