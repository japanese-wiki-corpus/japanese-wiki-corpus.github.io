Minosuke BANDO (坂東蓑助)

"Minosuke BANDO" is a professional name used by Kabuki (a traditional form of drama and music performed by male actors) actors. This name is often used before the actor uses the name of Mitsugoro BANDO. The yago (stage family name) used by Minosuke BANDO is Yamato-ya.
