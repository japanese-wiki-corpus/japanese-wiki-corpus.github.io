Kintaru OGIMACHISANJO (正親町三条公厚)

Kintaru OGIMACHISANJO (February 15, 1820 - February 3, 1822) was a retainer of the Imperial Court during the latter half of the Edo period. His father was Sangi (councillor) Saneyosi OGIMACHISANJO. His mother was the daughter of Mitsutsura MATSUDAIRA, the lord of Matsumoto Domain. His younger brother was Dainagon (chief councilor of state) Sanenaru OGIMACHISANJO.

He was one of the family heads of the Ogimachisanjo family, however, he died at the age of three. His last official rank was Jugoinoge (Junior Fifth Rank, Lower Grade).
