Kantaro NAKAMURA (中村勘太郎)

Kantaro NAKAMURA was the stage name of several Kabuki actors. The name of the lineage (ya) was the Nakamura-ya. The Nakamura family crest was an octagon adorned with ginkgo leaves.

Kantaro NAKAMURA (founder of the Nakamura lineage)
- He was the eldest son of the family founder Kankuro NAKAMURA.

Kantaro NAKAMURA II
- He was the eldest son of Kanzaburo NAKAMURA XVIII.
