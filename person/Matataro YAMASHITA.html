Matataro YAMASHITA (山下又太郎)

Matataro YAMASHITA is a hereditary family name for an actor of Kabuki, a Japanese traditional theatrical drama.

For Kabuki actors, the name 'Matataro' is used in two different hereditary family names 'Matataro YAMASHITA' and 'Matataro BANDO.'

Matataro YAMASHITA (I)
the second son of Ayame YOSHIZAWA (I)

Matataro YAMASHITA (II)
