Hiroto TADOKORO (田所弘人)

Hiroto TADOKORO (year of birth and death unknown) was a member of Mibu Roshi (masterless samurai group of Mibu) and also a member the Shinsengumi (a Tokugawa shogunate police force located in Kyoto).

He joined the corps in around June 1863, and his name was on the 'Bakufu Teishutsu Josho Shomeisha Ichiran' (List of Signers for Official Documents Submitted to the Shogunate) which was submitted on July 10, 1863. In the same year, he participated in the Coup of August 18. It is thought that he deserted the corps by the time that the Ikedaya Incident occurred.
