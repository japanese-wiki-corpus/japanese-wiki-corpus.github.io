Sakon ONOE (尾上左近)

Sakon ONOE is the name of a Kabuki actor lineage. The family's stage name is Otowaya.

Tatsunosuke ONOE I

- He was the eldest son of Shoroku ONOE II. He later took the name Tatsunosuke ONOE I. After he died, the name of Shoroku ONOE III was bestowed on him posthumously.

Sakon ONOE II

- He is the eldest son of the founder of the lineage. Today, he is known as Shoroku ONOE IV.
