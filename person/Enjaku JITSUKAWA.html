Enjaku JITSUKAWA (實川延若)

Enjaku JITSUKAWA is a professional name for kabuki actor. The stage family name is Kawachiya. Jomon (family crest) is Kasaneizutsu and kaemon (alternate personal crest) is Itsutsukarigane. Enjaku' is originated from the Haimyo (also known as Haimei; the term originally refers to a pen name as a haiku poet, but it also refers to a kabuki actor's offstage name which can be used officially and privately) of the former master Gakujuro JITSUKAWA.

Enjaku JITSUKAWA (the first)
Adopted son of the owner of Shibaijaya (a tea house located within a theater), Shobe KAWACHIYA, and his real father was a carpenter in Osaka; 1831 - 1885.
