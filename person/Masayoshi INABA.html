Masayoshi INABA (稲葉正誼)

Masayoshi INABA (March 28, 1827 - November 17, 1848) was the 11th lord of Yodo Domain of the Yamashiro Province. He was the 15th grand master of the Inaba family with ties to Masanari.

He was the seventh son of Masanori SAKAKIBARA, the Lord of the Takada Domain in the Echigo Province. His rank was Jugoinoge (Junior Fifth Rank, Lower Grade). He was Tango no kuni no kami (the governor of Tango Province).

He was born on March 28, 1827. He took over as head of the family on August 25, 1842. On November 17, 1848, he died at age 22, and his adopted heir Masakuni INABA succeeded.
