Yunoshin TAKAHASHI (高梁雄之進)

Yunoshin TAKAHASHI (born 1835, year of death unknown) was a soldier in the shinsengumi.

He was from Sagami Province.

He joined the shinsengumi around August of 1864.

He joined the Kimmon no Hen (Conspiracy of Kimmon).

After the death of Sanjuro TANI, he disappeared.

There was no news of his whereabouts for approximately ten years. In May 1877, he met with Hajime SAITO.

It is said that he died soon thereafter of an illness, but there is no proof to substantiate the claim.
