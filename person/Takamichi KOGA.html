Takamichi KOGA (久我堯通)

Takamichi KOGA (1608 - April 17, 1642) was a retainer of Imperial Court during the early Edo period. He was ranked at Shogoinoge (Senior Fifth Rank, Lower Grade) Sakone no shosho (Minor Captain of the Left Division of Inner Palace Guards). His father was Michisaki KOGA. His mother was unknown. His adopted child was Hiromichi KOGA.
