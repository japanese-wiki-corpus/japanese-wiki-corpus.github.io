Nobutaka KONPARU (金春信高)

Nobutaka KONPARU (1920 -) is a Noh actor of the Konparu school of shite-kata (lead actors). The 79th head of the Konparu school.

He was born in Nara as the eldest son of Hachijo (Mitsutaro) KONPARU, the 78th head of the school. He moved his base to Tokyo in 1956.

Yasuaki KONPARU is his eldest son. Kinzo KONPARU is his younger brother.
