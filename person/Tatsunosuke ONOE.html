Tatsunosuke ONOE (尾上辰之助)

Tatsunosuke ONOE is a Kabuki (traditional drama performed by male actors) actor. The stage family name is Otowaya. The Jomon (family crest) is Yotsu-wa ni Daki-gashiwa (four rings with a pair of Japanese emperor leaves).

Tatsunosuke ONOE (the first): The oldest son of Shoroku ONOE (the second).

The oldest son of Shoroku ONOE (the second). After his death, he was conferred the name of Shoroku ONOE (the third) posthumously.

Tatsunosuke ONOE (the second): The oldest son of the first.

The oldest son of the first. He is present Shoroku ONOE (the fourth).
