Toshisada BOJO (坊城俊定)

Toshisada BOJO (1251 or 1252 - December 25, 1310) was a court noble in the Kamakura period. He was the originator of the Bojo clan. His father was Tsunetoshi YOSHIDA. His mother was a daughter of TAIRA no Narimitsu. His Buddhist name was Bojo Dainagon (Major Counselor).

Brief Personal History

He was born as a son of Tsunetoshi YOSHIDA in 1251. He established the Bojo family later.

He became a Sangi (councillor) on July 11, 1288.

He became a priest on August 28, 1306.

He died in 1310.
