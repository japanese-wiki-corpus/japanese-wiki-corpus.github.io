Takamoto ANEKOJI (姉小路高基)

Takamoto ANEKOJI (1298 - April 18, 1358) was a court noble from the Kamakura period to the period of the Northern and Southern Courts (Japan). His father was FUJIWARA no Yorimoto. His brothers included Ietsuna ANEKOJI, Mototada ANEKOJI and Yoritoki ANEKOJI. He served in Hida Province, as Kunaikyo (Ministry of the Sovereign's Household) and Sangi (Royal Advisor). He was awarded the rank of Jusanmi (Junior Third Rank). He died on April 18, 1358. He died at the age of 62.
