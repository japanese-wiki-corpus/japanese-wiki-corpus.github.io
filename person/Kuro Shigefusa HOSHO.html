Kuro Shigefusa HOSHO (宝生九郎重英)

Kuro Shigefusa HOSHO (July 2, 1900 - July 18, 1974) was a Noh actor of the Hosho school of shite-kata (lead actors). The 17th head of the Hosho school.

He was a son of Kuro Tomoharu HOSHO, the 16th head of the school. He was a member of the Japan Art Academy. He trained many capable disciples. Fusao HOSHO was his son.
