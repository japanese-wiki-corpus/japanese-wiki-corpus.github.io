Shironaga TSUTSUMI (堤代長)

Shironaga TSUTSUMI (March 13, 1716 - December 21, 1783) was a Kugyo (high court noble) in the Edo period. He was the seventh head of the Tsutsumi family, which was Meike (or Meika) (the fourth highest status for court nobles). His foster father was the sixth head of the Tsutsumi family, Harunaga TSUTSUMI. His biological father was Toshikiyo BOJO. His mother was a daughter of Terunaga TSUTSUMI, the third head of the Tsutsumi family. His lawful wife was the daughter of Korenaga TAKENOUCHI. Yukinaga TSUTSUMI, Chozan WAKAE (adopted heir of the Wakae family), Yorihiro HAMURO were his sons.
