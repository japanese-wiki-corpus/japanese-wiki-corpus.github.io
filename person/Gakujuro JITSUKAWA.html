Gakujuro JITSUKAWA (實川額十郎)

Gakujuro JITSUKAWA was a Kabuki performer. His stage family name (Kabuki) was Izutsuya.

Gakujuro JITSUKAWA (the first)

He was a disciple of (the first) Kuzaemon ASAO.

Gakujuro JITSUKAWA (the second)

He was adopted to the first Gakujuro JITSUKAWA.
He was commonly called 'Gakujuro the Blind.'
