Fuyumune OINOMIKADO (大炊御門冬宗)

Fuyumune OINOMIKADO (1357-June 10, 1405) was Kugyo (top court official) who lived from the period of the Northern and Southern Courts to the Muromachi period. He served two Emperors, Emperor Chokei (the 98th) and the Emperor Gokameyama (the 99th), and was promoted to Juichii (Junior 1st Rank) Dainagon (chief councilor of state). His father was Fuyunobu OINOMIKADO. His elder brothers were Munezane OINOMIKADO and Nobutsune OINOMIKADO. His child was Muneuji OINOMIKADO.

He became Jusanmi (Junior 3rd Rank) and was raised to Kugyo in 1368. After experience as Mutsu-no-Gonnokami (a supernumerary chief of Mutsu-no-kuni), Sangi (councilor) and Gon Chunagon (a provisional vice-councilor of state), he became Gon Dainagon (a provisional major councilor) in 1382. Though he resigned Gon Dainagon in 1385, he reassumed in 1402. He resigned the next year. Though he became Juichii in 1405, he died.
