Hashinosuke NAKAMURA (中村橋之助)

Hashinosuke NAKAMURA is one of the professional names of Kabuki (traditional performing art) actors.

The first Hashinosuke NAKAMURA
He was later to become Shirobe NAKAMURA (the former second Utashichi KAGAYA)

The second Hashinosuke NAKAMURA
He was later to become Tsurusuke NAKAMURA the Fifth. He was a child of the third Shikan NAKAMURA.

Hashinosuke NAKAMURA the Third
