Tametsugi REIZEI (冷泉為系)

Tametsugi REIZEI (1881 - 1946) was a Japanese poet. He was the twenty-second head of the Kami Reizei family. His father was Tamemoto REIZEI. His wife was Kyoko, a daughter of Tadasuke MINASE. His children were Tameomi REIZEI and Fumiko REIZEI. His son-in-law was Tametou REIZEI.
