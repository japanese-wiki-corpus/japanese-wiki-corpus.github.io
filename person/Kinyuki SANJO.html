Kinyuki SANJO (三条公行)

Kinyuki SANJO (1105 - July 16, 1148) was a Kugyo (top court official) at the end of the Heian period. He was part of the Kanin line of the Northern House of the Fujiwara clan. He was the second son of Saneyuki SANJO. He was Jusanmi (Junior Third Rank) and Sangi (councillor).

Career
1122: Uhyoe no suke (Assistant Captain of the Right Division of Middle Palace Guards)
1124: Jugoinojo (Junior Fifth Rank, Upper Grade)
1126: Shogoinoge (Senior Fifth Rank, Lower Grade) and Sashosho (Minor Captain of the Left Division of the Inner Palace Guards)
1131: Suo no suke (Assistant Governor of Suo Province) and Sashoben (Third-rank Officer of the Left)
1132: Uchuben (Middle Controller of the Right)
1133: Jushiinoge (Junior Forth Rank, Lower Grade)
1134: Jushiijo (Junior Fourth Rank, Upper Grade), Gon Sachuben (Provisional Middle Controller of the Left), and Shoshiinoge (Senior Fourth Rank, Lower Grade)
1135: Sachuben (Middle Controller of the Left)
1136: Concurrently Bizen no suke (Assistant Governor of Bizen Province) and Kurodo no to (Head Chamberlain)
1138: Sangi (councilor)
1139: Concurrently Harima gon no kami (Provisional Governor of Harima Province)
1141: Concurrently Uhyoe no kami (Captain of the Right Division of the Middle Palace Guards)
1142: Jusanmi (Junior Third Rank)
1144: Concurrently Echizen no Gon no kami (Provisional Governor of Echizen Province)
