Shinshichi ONOE (尾上新七)

Shinshichi ONOE is a hereditary name of a line of traditional Japanese Kabuki drama actors.

A pupil of Kikugoro ONOE (First generation)

Shinshichi ONOE (II)

Shinshichi ONOE (III) (year of birth and death unknown/died circa 1868)

Son of Omezo ICHIKAWA (II)

Shinshichi ONOE (IV) (year of birth and death unknown)

A pupil of Kikugoro ONOE (V)
