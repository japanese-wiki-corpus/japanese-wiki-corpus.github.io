Narimichi NIJO (二条斉通)

Narimichi NIJO (May 31, 1781 - July 4, 1798) was kugyo (high court noble) in the Edo period. His father was Harutaka NIJO. His mother was Yoshihime (嘉姫) (daughter of Munemoto TOKUGAWA). His court official rank was Junii (Junior Second Rank). His alias was Kyotokuin (恭徳院).

Brief Personal History

He was born to Harutaka NIJO in Kyoto in 1781. He was appointed to Jusanmi (Junior Third Rank) on February 22, 1791 (old calendar). He held various posts such as Naidaijin (Minister of the Center) (1797 - 1798). He died in 1798.
