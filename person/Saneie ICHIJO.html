Saneie ICHIJO (一条実家)

Saneie ICHIJO (1250 - February 20, 1314) was Kugyo (a Court noble) in the Kamakura period. His father was Sanetsune ICHIJO. His brothers included Ietsune ICHIJO, Moronaga ICHIJO, Tadasuke ICHIJO and Iefusa ICHIJO. His go (pen name) was Ichijo, 又号町. He was Juichii (Junior First Rank).

Brief Personal History

He was born in 1250 as the second son of Sanetsune ICHIJO. He was conferred Jusanmi (Junior Third Rank) in December 2, 1267. He held various posts including Naidaijin (a minister) (1305 - 1306) and Daijo-daijin (Grand Minister) (1306 - 1309). He died in 1314.
