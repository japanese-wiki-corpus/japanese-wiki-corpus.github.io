FUJIWARA no Nariie (藤原成家)

FUJIWARA no Nariie(藤原 成家, 1155 - July 12, 1220) was a Kugyo (high court noble) in the early Kamakura period. He was the oldest son of FUJIWARA no Toshinari (Shunzei) of the Mikohidari school. He was ranked as Shosanmi Hyobukyo (Senior Third Rank, Minister for Military Affairs).

Career

In 1157, he was raised to the peerage for the first time, and in 1167, he was appointed as Jiju (Chamberlain). In 1185, he was appointed as Ukone no shosho (Minor Captain of the Right Division of Inner Palace Guards), and in 1199, his position was changed to Ukone no chujo (Middle Captain of the Right Division of Inner Palace Guards). In 1203, he was appointed as Jusanmi (Junior Third Rank), and in 1210, he was promoted to Shosanmi, and in 1211, he was appointed as Hyobukyo, but in 1215, he became a priest.
