Tsunemichi NIJO (二条経通)

Tsunemichi NIJO (1255 - date of death unknown) was a kuge (court noble) of the Kamakura period. He was the fourth son of Yoshizane NIJO. His mother is not known. Some of his siblings were Michinaga NIJO, Morotada NIJO, and Kanemoto NIJO, and Norinaga NIJO. He was a Senior Second Rank. Non-Councilor.

Brief Personal History

In 12５5, Tsunemichi was born as the fourth son of Yoshizane NIJO in Kyoto. On August 16, 1265, he received Junior Third Rank. He entered priesthood in December 1296. His age at death is unknown.
