Shigeyoshi NIJO (二条重良)

Shigeyoshi NIJO (December 25, 1751 - August 13, 1768) was a court noble in the Edo period. His father was Munemoto NIJO. He served as Gon Dainagon (provisional major counselor) with the court official rank of Shonii (Senior Second Rank). His alias was Isokuin (維則院).

Brief Personal History

He was born to Munemoto NIJO in 1751. He was appointed to Jusanmi (Junior Third Rank) on January 9, 1759 (old calendar). He died in 1768.
