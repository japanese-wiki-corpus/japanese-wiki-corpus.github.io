MIWA no Fumiya (三輪文屋)

MIWA no Fumiya (the year of birth unknown - 643?) was a person in the Asuka period. His kabane (hereditary title) was Kimi.

He served for Prince YAMASHIRO no Oe who was Prince Shotoku's son; He ran up to Mt. Ikoma with Prince YAMASHIRO no Oe when they were attacked at the Jogu Oke family. Nevertheless, it is said that he committed suicide due to this affair.

His father was MIWA no Kosazaki; He had some children including MIWA no Toshikane.
