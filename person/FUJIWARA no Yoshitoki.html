FUJIWARA no Yoshitoki (藤原善時)

FUJIWARA no Yoshitoki (year of birth and death unknown) was a nobleman approximately during the mid Heian period. He successively held the position of Assistant governor of Musashi Province. In the Anna Incident in March 969, he worked with Sama no suke (vice-minister of Left Division of Bureau of Horses) MINAMOTO no Mitsunaka to overthrow Sadaijin (minister of the left) MINAMOTO no Takaakira and others.
