Mihashira no Uzuno Miko (三貴子)

Mihashira no Uzuno Miko refers to final three deities that came into the world when Izanagi came back from Yominokuni (Hades) and cleansed dirtiness from the Yomi (world after death), according to the Kiki-shinwa (the Kojiki, Nihonshoki and mythology). It is also called Sankishi or Sankishin.

Amaterasu
- A goddess born from the left eye of Izanagi. The sun goddess.

Tsukuyomi
- A god born from the right eye of Izanagi (Some say it is a goddess). The moon god governing night.

Susanoo
- A god born from the nose of Izanagi. The sea god.
