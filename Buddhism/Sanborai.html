Sanborai (三宝礼)

Sanborai is a gemon taken from Zunshi Jodosangangi.

Gemon

一心敬礼十方法界常住仏 (Isshin kyorai jippo hokai jojubu)
一心敬礼十方法界常住法 (Isshin kyorai jippo hokai jojuho)
一心敬礼十方法界常住僧 (Isshin kyorai jippo hokai jojuso)

Translation

With devoted respect, I worship Buddha living in Jippohokai (all of the existing world). With devoted respect, I worship the laws of Buddhism living in Jippohokai. With devoted respect, I worship the monks living in Jippohokai.
