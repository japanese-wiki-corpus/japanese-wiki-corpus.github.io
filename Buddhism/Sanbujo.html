Sanbujo (三奉請)

Sanbujo is a gemon taken from Hojisan (Hymns of the Pure Land Ritual).

Gemon

奉請弥陀如来 (Bujomidanyorai) 入道場 (Nyudojo) 散華楽 (Sangeraku)
奉請釈迦如来 (Bujoshakanyorai) 入道場 (Nyudojo) 散華楽 (Sangeraku)
奉請十方如来 (Bujojipponyorai) 入道場 (Nyudojo) 散華楽 (Sangeraku)

Translation

Adored Amida Nyorai, please enter the dojo, where I will scatter flowers to receive you. Adored Shaka Nyorai (Shakyamuni), please enter the dojo, where I will scatter flowers to receive you. Adored saints from everywhere, please enter the dojo, where I will scatter flowers to receive you.
