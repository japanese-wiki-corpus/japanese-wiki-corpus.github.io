Shoyakumon (摂益文)

Shoyakumon is a gemon included in Kanmuryoju-kyo shaku.

Gemon
光明徧照 (Komyo henjo)
十方世界 (Jippo sekai)
念仏衆生 (Nenbutsu shujo)
摂取不捨 (Sesshu fusha)

Translation of gemon
Light from Nyorai
illuminates all over the world,
by which all creatures that chant Buddhist sutra
are saved and not reprobated.
