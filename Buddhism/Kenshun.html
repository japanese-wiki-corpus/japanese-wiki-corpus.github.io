Kenshun (兼俊)

Kenshun (962 - year of death unclear) was a priest of the Shingon sect in the late Heian period.
His father was FUJIWARA no Kaneie
His azana (popular name) was Enrin.

He received the Denpo-Kanjo (the consecration for the Transmission of the Dharma) in 1006 at the Hokaku-ji Temple on Mt. Kongo in Yamato Province. From then on, he practiced Kanpo (meditative training to reach enlightenment) and devoted himself to attaining miracles.
