Michinokuchinokihe no kunimiyatsuko (道口岐閉国造)

Michinokuchinokihe no kunimiyatsuko 道口岐閉国造 (also known as Michinokuchinokihekokkuzo) was kuninomiyatsuko (local ruling families in ancient Japan) ruled the northern edge of Hitachi Province. It is also written as 道尻岐閉国造 or 岐閇国造.

Ancestor

Takekoro no mikoto. His son Usahinone was appointed Michinokuchinokuchinokihe no kunimiyatsuko during the reign of Emperor Ojin.

Base

Michinokuchi Go, Taka Gun, Hitachi Province.
