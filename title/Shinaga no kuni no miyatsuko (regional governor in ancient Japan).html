Shinaga no kuni no miyatsuko (regional governor in ancient Japan) (師長国造)

Shinaga no kuni no miyatsuko (師長国造), also known as Shinaga kokuzo, was a kuni no miyatsuko ruled the west part of Sagami Province. It is also Witten as 磯長国造.

Ancestor

Takekoro no mikoto. The 14th descendant from Amatsuhikone no mikoto. Oowashiomi no mikoto, a son of Takekoro no mikoto, was appointed Shinaganokuni no miyatsuko during the reign of Emperor Seimu. He was descended from the same line that includes Takaichi no agatanushi, Ibarakino kuni no miyatsuko, and the Saegusa clan.

Clan

Unknwon.

Base

Shinaga Go, Yorogi no Kori, Sagami Province. The present Oisomachi, Kanagawa Prefecture.

Local tutelar deity

Unknwon. The area includes Kawasa-jinja Shrine, an very old shrine.
