Naka no kuninomiyatsuko (仲国造)

Naka no kuninomiyatsuko 仲国造 (also known as Nakakokuzo) was kuninomiyatsuko (local ruling families in ancient Japan) ruled the east part of Hitachi Province. It was also written as 那珂国造, 那賀国造, and 常道仲国造.

Ancestor

Kamuyaimimi no mikoto. It is said that the descendant Takekashima no mikoto was appointed Naka no kuninomiyatsuko during the reign of Emperor Seimu.

Clan

The Mibu clan.

Base

Naka Gun, Hitachi Province. Present Nakaminato City, Katsuta City and the like in Ibaraki Prefecture.

Local tutelar deity

It was probably the Kshima-jingu Shrine.

Descendants

Oodate Mibunoonoko: A legendary person during the Kofun period.
