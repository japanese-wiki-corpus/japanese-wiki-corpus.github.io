Keijina (Kyoto, Shiga and Nara Prefectures) (京滋奈)

Keijina is a term referring to the area combining Kyoto Prefecture, Shiga Prefecture, and Nara Prefecture. It also refers to the areas south of Funai County in Kyoto Prefecture, Konan region in Shiga Prefecture, and Nara Basin.
