Kanagamisaki Cape (金ヶ岬)

Kanagamisaki Cape belongs to Maizuru City, Kyoto Prefecture, and faces the Japan Sea (western part of Wakasa Bay).

It is located in the east of Kunda Bay on the western side of Maizuru Bay's mouth. It is part of the Wakasa Bay Quasi-National Park.

Geography

Kanagamisaki Cape juts into Wakasa Bay.

Its topography consists of a sheer cliff which rises up and faces the sea.
