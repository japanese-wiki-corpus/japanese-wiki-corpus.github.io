Naryu-misaki Cape (成生岬)

Naryu-misaki Cape belongs to Maizuru City, Kyoto Prefecture, and faces the Japan Sea (western part of Wakasa Bay). It is located at 35 degrees 36 minutes north latitude, and 135 degrees 28 minutes east longitude.

Naryu-misaki Cape is located at the northern end of Oura Peninsula, and at the northeastern extremity of Kyoto Prefecture. It is surrounded by a hilly district 100 to 200m above sea level, with the Naryuzaki lighthouse situated on its edge. The cliffs of Naryu-misaki Cape keep people away, and no settlements exist. The Cape is also designated as Wakasa Bay Quasi-National Park.
