Imperial Prince Kuninobu FUSHIMINOMIYA (伏見宮邦房親王)

Imperial Prince Kuninobu FUSHIMINOMIYA (Mayl 3, 1566 - January 6, 1622) was a prince of imperial blood who lived from the Sengoku (warring states) period into the early Edo period. He was the Shikibukyo (Lord of the Ministry of Ceremonial). He was also the ninth head, in succession, of the Fushiminomiya family. Kuninobu was the third prince born to Imperial Prince Kunisuke FUSHIMINOMIYA, the seventh head, in succession, of the Fushiminomiya family.

He was raised to the rank of prince of imperial blood in an imperial proclamation dating to 1575.

Kuninobu's children of princely blood included Imperial Prince Sadakiyo FUSHIMINOMIYA.
