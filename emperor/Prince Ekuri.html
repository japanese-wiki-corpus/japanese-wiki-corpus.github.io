Prince Ekuri (殖栗皇子)

Prince Ekuri (578 - year of death unknown) was the fifth son of Emperor Yomei. His mother was ANAHOBE no Hashihitohime.

His career wasn't recorded at all. But he was with his elder brother Prince Shotoku and his nephew Prince Yamashiro no oe in Tohon Miei (Portrait of Prince Shotoku).
