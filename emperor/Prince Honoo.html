Prince Honoo (火焔皇子)

Prince Honoo (Also pronounced Honoo oji, Honoo no miko [date of birth and death unknown]). Prince Honoo was a member of the Imperial family in the Asuka period. He was also called Honoo no kimi and Imperial Prince Honoo. It is said that he lived in Settsu province and "Settsu-meisho-zue" (a book introducing beauty spots in Settsu) reported that his graveyard was in Itami city, but currently it is premise of Osaka International Airport and doesn't exist.
