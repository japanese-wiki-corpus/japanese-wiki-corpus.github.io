Soga no ishikawa no sukune (蘇我石川宿禰)

Soga no ishikawa no sukune, was a Japanese mythological figure, also called Soga no ishikawa, and he was said to be the ancestor of the Soga clan
He was born in Ishikawa County, Kawachi Province (Osaka Prefecture). He was a son of TAKENOUCHI no Sukune, and the father of Soga no machi. Also there is a theory that judging from the name, this figure was an imaginary person created by SOGANOKURA-YAMADA no Ishikawamaro or some of his descendents.
