Prince Isonokami (石上皇子)

Prince Isonokami (Also pronounced Isonokami no oji, Isonokami no miko [date of birth and death unknown])
Prince Isonokami was a member of the Imperial family in the Asuka period. His name was written Isonokami no miko (石上王) in "Kojiki" (The Records of Ancient Matters).
