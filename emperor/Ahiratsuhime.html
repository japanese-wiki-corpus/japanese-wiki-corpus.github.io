Ahiratsuhime (吾平津姫)

Ahiratsuhime (吾平津姫) was the wife of Emperor Jinmu. In "Kojiki" (The Records of Ancient Matters), she was mentioned as Ahirahime (阿比良比売). She was a younger sister of Atanoobashi. While Emperor Jinmu was living in Hyuga Province, she became the wife of Emperor Jinmu and had two sons, Tagishimimi no mikoto and Kisumimi no mikoto.
