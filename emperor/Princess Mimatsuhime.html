Princess Mimatsuhime (御真津比売命)

Princess Mimatsuhime (Mimatsuhime no Mikoto; the dates of her birth and death are unknown) was a princess of the Emperor Kaika. Princess Mimatsuhime (御真津比売命) was also written in this way, Mimatsuhime no Mikoto (御真津姫命). The mother of Princess Mimatsuhime was Ikagashikome no Mikoto. Emperor Sujin was his maternal half-brother.
