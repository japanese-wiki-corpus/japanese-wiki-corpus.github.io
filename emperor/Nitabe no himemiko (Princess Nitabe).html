Nitabe no himemiko (Princess Nitabe) (新田部皇女)

Nitabe no himemiko (year of birth unknown - October 26, 699) was a princess of the Emperor Tenchi. Her mother was Tachibana no iratsume (whose father was ABE no Uchimaro). Her older sister-uterine was Asuka no himemiko (the Princess Asuka). When she died in 699, an imperial edict ordered that the Imperial Family members, the vassals and all the officials to attend her funeral.
