TACHIBANA no Iratsume (橘娘)

TACHIBANA no Iratsume (year of birth unknown - March 26, 681) was a daughter of ABE no Kurahashimaro.

She married the Emperor Tenchi and had the Princess Asuka and the Princess Nitabe. Her sister was Otarashihime who married the Emperor Kotoku. Her name is also known as Abe no fujin (阿倍夫人).
