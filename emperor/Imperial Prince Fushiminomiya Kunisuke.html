Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Kunisuke (伏見宮邦輔親王)

Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Kunisuke (April 25, 1513 - April 18, 1563) was an Imperial family member during the Sengoku period (period of warring states). He was Shikibukyo (Chief of the Ministry of Ceremonies). The seventh family head of Fushiminomiya. The first Prince of Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Sadaatsu.
His mother was Saneka SANJO's daughter, Takako (Kaoriko/Koshi) FUJIWARA

He received the title of Imperial Prince in 1531 and had a coming-of-age ceremony in 1532.

The Prince's children were Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Sadayasu, Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Kuninobu, Cloistered Imperial Prince Join (Buddhist Priest), Cloistered Imperial Prince Soncho, Cloistered Imperial Prince Saiin.

He was laid to rest in Fushiminomiya cemetery in Kamigyo Ward, Kyoto City.
