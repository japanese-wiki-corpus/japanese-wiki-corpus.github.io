Princess Kunikatahime (国方姫命)

Princess Kunikatahime (Kunikatahime no Mikoto; the dates of her birth and death are unknown) was a princess of the Emperor Sujin. The mother of Princess Kunikatahime was the Empress Mimakihime.
The Emperor Suinin was a maternal half-brother of Princess Kunikatahime
Her paternal half-brothers were Prince Toyokiiribiko, Prince Yasakairibiko, and so on. Details of her (Princess Kunikatahime) achievements are unknown.
