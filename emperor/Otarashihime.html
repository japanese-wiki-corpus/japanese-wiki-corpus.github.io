Otarashihime (小足媛)

Otarashihime (year of birth and death unknown) was a daughter of ABE no Uchimaro.

She married the Emperor Kotoku and had the Prince Arima. Her sister was TACHIBANA no Iratsume who married the Emperor Tenchi.

It is reported that her name is also written as 男足媛 (Otarashihime). There is a description in the section of March, 644 of Nihonshoki (Chronicles of Japan), which is the Prince Karu made his favorite mistress, of the Abe clan to look after NAKATOMI no Kamako, who stayed the palace when the Prince Karu did not go to the Imperial Court; it is believed that this favorite mistress, of the Abe clan was Otarashihime.
