Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Sadayasu (伏見宮貞康親王)

Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Sadayasu (February 1, 1547 - May 11, 1568) was an Imperial family member during the Sengoku period (period of warring states). He was Shikibukyo (chief of the Ministry of Ceremonies). He was the eighth family head of Fushiminomiya family. He was the second Prince of Imperial Prince Fushiminomiya Kunisuke. His mother was Sanenobu SAIONJI's daughter.

He received the title of Imperial Prince in 1563 and had a coming-of-age ceremony.

His mausoleum is in Fushiminomiya cemetery in Kamigyo Ward, Kyoto City.
