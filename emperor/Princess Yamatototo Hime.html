Princess Yamatototo Hime (倭迹迹姫命)

Princess Yamatototo Hime (Yamatototo Hime no Mikoto; the dates of her birth and death unknown) was the first princess of the Emperor Kogen. The mother of Princess Yamatototo Hime was Utsushikome no Mikoto (she was the Empress of the Emperor Kogen). Prince Obiko, Prince Sukunahiko Takeigokoro, and Emperor Kaika were her maternal half-brothers. There are no written records of her (Princess Yamatototo Hime) achievements.
