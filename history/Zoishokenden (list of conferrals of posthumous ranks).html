Zoishokenden (list of conferrals of posthumous ranks) (贈位諸賢伝)

"Zoishokenden" is the title of a book which lists 966 persons who were granted a rank posthumously between 1868 and 1911; a brief biography is provided for each of them. The people whose names appeared in this book were mainly scholars, statesmen and patriots from the end of the Edo period.
