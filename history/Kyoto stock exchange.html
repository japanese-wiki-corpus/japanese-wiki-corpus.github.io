Kyoto stock exchange (京都証券取引所)

The Kyoto stock exchange was abolished on March 1st, 2001.

The Osaka stock exchange took over companies listed on the Kyoto stock exchange when it was abolished. Only three companies were listed solely on the Kyoto stock exchange: Goo chemical, Kyoto hotel, and Mitani Shindo.
