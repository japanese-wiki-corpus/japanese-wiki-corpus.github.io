Kyoto Rekishi Kairo Kyogikai (京都歴史回廊協議会)

Kyoto Rekishi Kairo Kyogikai (Kyoto "Historic Corridor" Conference) is a Kyoto civil activity. The main members of the conference are residents and academics from universities in the north-western part of Kyoto City where there are a large number of historical heritage sites, such as temples, shrines, and Emperor tombs. These members study and work on the preservation of regional history, cultural heritage, and nature, as well as being engaged in human resource development in these fields.

Executive Office

Historic Corridor Promotion Office, Department of Education and Culture, Gakko Hojin Ritsumeikan

Participating Universities

Kyoto Saga University of Arts
Hanazono University
Bukkyo University
Ritsumeikan University
