Renjo-ji Temple (Yasu City) (蓮乗寺 (野洲市))

Renjo-ji Temple is a temple of the Tendai Shinseishu sect located in Yasu City, Shiga Prefecture (former Chozu-cho, Yasu county). Sango (the temple's title) is Seiryu-san. The principal image is Amida Nyorai.

History
The temple is said to have been founded by Zengen in 824 and restored during 1501-1504.

Cultural Property
Important cultural property
Wooden Bishamon-ten ryuzo (wooden standing statue of the God of Treasure, War)

Address
1191, Hie, Yasu City, Shiga Prefecture
