Kawata-jinja Shrine (Minakuchi-cho, Koga City) (川田神社 (甲賀市水口町))

Kawata-jinja Shrine is a Shinto shrine located in Minakuchi-cho, Koga City, Shiga Prefecture. It is said that the shrine was established in the reign of Emperor Suinin. It is a Myojin-taisha Shrine listed in Engishiki (an ancient book for codes and procedures on national rites and prayers). The shrine was given a shinkai (ranks granted to shinto deity) of Shoichii (Senior First Rank) in 1726. It is the soja (representative shrine in the area) of Kuratasho (Kurata area). It was listed as a sonsha (a village shrine) in 1876, a gosha (a regional shrine) in 1884 and promoted to a kensha (a prefectural shrine) in 1936.
