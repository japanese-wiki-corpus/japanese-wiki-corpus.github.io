Myoju-ji Temple (Gojo City) (妙住寺 (五條市))

Myoju-ji Temple is a temple of the Nichiren-shoshu sect located in Gojo City, Nara Prefecture. Its sango (honorific mountain prefix) is Jodosan.

Origin and History

December 22, 1973 - it was founded. The patron of the temple in its founding was Nittatsu Shonin, the 66th head priest of Nichiren-shoshu Taiseki-ji Temple.

December 10, 1981 - the ossuary was built.

Address

2-23, 5 Chome, Futami, Gojo City, Nara Prefecture

Surrounding Area

Yoshino River

Saito Lumber Company

Gojo-Futami Post Office

Access

Ten-minute walk from Yamato-Futami Station on Wakayama line
