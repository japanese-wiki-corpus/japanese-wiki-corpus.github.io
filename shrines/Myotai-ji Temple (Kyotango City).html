Myotai-ji Temple (Kyotango City) (妙泰寺 (京丹後市))

Myotai-ji Temple is a temple of the Nichiren Shoshu sect located in Kyotango City, Kyoto Prefecture. Its Sango (literally, "mountain name"), which is the title prefixed to the name of a Buddhist temple, is Shintokusan.

June 9, 1982

Establishment. The 67th Hossu (head priest) of the Nichiren Shoshu sect Taiseki-ji Temple, Nikken Shonin (The Venerable Nikken) was Kaiki (patron of a temple at its foundation).
