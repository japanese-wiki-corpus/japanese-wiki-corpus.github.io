Shakazan Daibodai-ji Temple (釈迦山大菩提寺)

Shakazan Daibodai-ji Temple is the Agon Sect main temple located in Yamashina Ward, Kyoto City, Kyoto Prefecture.

The 'Agon no Hoshi Matsuri' fire festival is held on February 11 each year. Kagyu-an (Snail Cottage) within the temple garden is the venue for the 'Agon Cup' Go competition.
