Enjyu-ji Temple (Hikone City) (延寿寺 (彦根市))

Enjyu-ji Temple is a Zen temple (a temple belonging to the Zen sect) of Eigenji school of the Rinzai sect in Hikone City, Shiga Prefecture. The sango (literally "mountain name," the title prefixed to the name of a Buddhist temple) is 永明山. The honzon (principal image of Buddha) is Kanzeon Bosatsu.

History
This temple is said to have been founded by Gyogi in 749. Although it used to belong to the Tendai sect, in the Edo period it changed to the Rinzai sect.
