Modoroki-jinja Shrine (還来神社)

Modoroki-jinja Shrine is a Shinto shrine located in Ikadachitochu-cho, Otsu City, Shiga Prefecture.

The enshrined deity

The spirit of FUJIWARA no Tabiko

The crest for the shrine

Sagarifuji crest

History

It is unknown when the shrine was established.

Festival

May 6, Reisai (regular festival)

Shrines in precincts

Hiyoshi-jinja Shrine

Wakamiya-jinja Shrine

Geshi-jinja Shrine

Merits of the deity

Safe trip

Safety of one's family
