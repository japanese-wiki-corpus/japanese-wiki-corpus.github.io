Zuiko-ji Temple (Kyoto City) (瑞光寺 (京都市))

Zuiko-ji Temple is a Buddhist temple belonging to the Nichiren Sect located in Fushimi Ward, Kyoto City. It is also known as Gensei-an Temple.

History
The temple began as a thatched hut erected on this site by Nissei (Motomasa) in 1655.
