Honkan-ji Temple (本感寺)

Honkan-ji Temple is a temple of the Nichiren Shoshu sect located in Kameoka City, Kyoto Prefecture. Its Sango (literally, "mountain name"), which is the title prefixed to the name of a Buddhist temple, is Myoshusan.

July 20, 1989

Establishment. The 67th Hossu (head priest) of the Nichiren Shoshu sect Taiseki-ji Temple, Nikken Shonin (The Venerable Nikken) was Kaiki (patron of a temple at its foundation).
