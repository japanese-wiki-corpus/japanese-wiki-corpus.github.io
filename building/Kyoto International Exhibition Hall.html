Kyoto International Exhibition Hall (京都市勧業館)

Kyoto International Exhibition Hall is an event site located in Sakyo-ku Ward of Kyoto City, Kyoto Prefecture. It was designed by architect Kiyoshi KAWASAKI.

It is commonly called 'Miyako Messe.'

Operated by:

Kyoto Industrial Promotion Center Corporation

Location

9-1 Okazaki Seishoji-cho, Sakyo-ku Ward, Kyoto City

Phone: 075-762-2630

Access

It is about ten minutes on foot from Higashiyama Station of Kyoto City Subway Tozai Line.

Heian-jingu Shrine
Okazaki-koen Park
The National Museum of Modern Art, Kyoto
