Kuriyama Family Residence (the Japan's oldest private residence in Nara Prefecture) (栗山家住宅)

The Kuriyama Family Residence, which is located in 1-chome, Gojo, Gojo city, Nara Prefecture, is the Japan's oldest private residence of all the residences whose dates of building are known. During the time of innovation it became known from the ridge plates that the residence was built in 1607.

It is an important cultural property, but closed to the public since it is still used as a residence.
