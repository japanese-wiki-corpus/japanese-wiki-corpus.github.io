The Garden of Heian-jingu Shrine (平安神宮神苑)

The garden of Heian-jingu Shrine is a modern Japanese garden of the chisen kaiyu style (a style of Japanese garden with a path around a central pond) in Sakyo Ward, Kyoto City, Kyoto Prefecture. It consists of Minami (south) Shin-en Garden, Nishi (west) Shin-en Garden, Naka (middle) Shin-en Garden, and Higashi (east) Shin-en Garden that enclose the three sides behind Heian-jingu Shrine Daigokuden. It has an area of 33,000 square meters, was designed by Jihei OGAWA, and it has been nationally designated a place of scenic beauty.
