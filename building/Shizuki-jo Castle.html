Shizuki-jo Castle (指月城)

Shizuki-jo Castle is a Japanese castle.

Shizuki-jo Castle (also called Shigetsu-jo Castle) is Shizuki-san (mountain) Fushimi-jo Castle, which was in Fushimi Ward, Kyoto City.

Shizuki-jo Castle is another name for Hagi-jo Castle, which was in Hagi City, Yamaguchi Prefecture.
