Takase-gawa River Ichi no Funairi (高瀬川一之船入)

Takase-gawa River Ichi no Funairi (an artificially generated cove) is a cove in the Takase-gawa River (in Kyoto Prefecture) that provides a pier for boats.

This place was designated as a national historical site on January 22, 1934.

Location

Ichinofunairi-cho, Nishigawa, Nijo Sagaru, Kiyamachi-dori Street, Nakagyo-ku Ward, Kyoto City
