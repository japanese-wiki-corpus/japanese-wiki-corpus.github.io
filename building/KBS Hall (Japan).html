KBS Hall (Japan) (KBSホール (日本))

KBS Hall is a hall in the Broadcasting Hall of Kyoto Broadcasting System Co. Ltd. (KBS Kyoto) in Kamigyo Ward, Kyoto City. Stained-glass panels are installed inside the facility. Seating capacity is about 1000 seats. The hall runs the KBS Culture Center and features professional wrestling performances including Pro-Wrestling Noah and Dragon Gate games.
