The Seika Garden City (せいかガーデンシティ)

The Seika garden city, located in Hosono Nishi, Seika-cho Town, Soraku-gun County, Kyoto Prefecture, is a shopping center in front of Hosono Station on the JR Katamachi line (the science city line) and in front of Shin Hosono Station on the Kintetsu Kyoto line. It's preliminary opening was on May 16, 2007, and grand opening on May 18, 2007. SEED Co., Ltd. Operates the facility.
