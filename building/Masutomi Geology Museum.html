Masutomi Geology Museum (益富地学会館)

Masutomi Geology Museum is a foundation located in Kamigyo Ward, Kyoto City. It operates a museum of stones.

Summary
History
In 1973, it was inaugurated as Japan Geologic Study Hall. In 1991, the hall became a foundation and its name was changed to Masutomi Geology Museum.
Major business: the operation of a museum of stones (The Wonder Stone Museum)
Location: 394 Naka-demizu-cho, Demizu-dori Karasuma-nishi-iru, Kamigyo Ward, Kyoto City, Kyoto Prefecture
