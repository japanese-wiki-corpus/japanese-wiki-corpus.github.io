Kyoto Prefectural Gymnasium (京都府立体育館)

Kyoto Prefectural Gymnasium is a gymnasium located in Kita Ward, Kyoto City, Kyoto Prefecture.

Facility

The gymnasium opened October 10 (Health Sports Day), 1971. The main stadium has a seating capacity of 8635. It is used for concerts and official games of volleyball and basketball.

Access

Six minutes walk from Enmachi Station, JR Sanin Main Line.
Six minutes walk from Daishogun Bus Stop
