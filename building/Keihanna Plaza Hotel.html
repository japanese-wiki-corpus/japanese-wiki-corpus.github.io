Keihanna Plaza Hotel (けいはんなプラザホテル)

Keihanna Plaza Hotel is an accommodation facility within Keihanna Plaza which is the central intersecting facility of Kansai Science City.

At first, it was managed as the Keihanna Miyako Hotel, a part of Miyako Hotels and Resorts. Miyako Hotels and Resorts later withdrew from the restaurant industry, thus it remains as Keihanna Plaza Hotel with only the accomodation industry.

In the surrounding area
Gakkentoshi Exhibition Pavilion
Kansai-kan of the National Diet Library
Musee Louis Leloup Museum
The Vocational Museum
Keihanna Commemorative Park

Access
Approximately 10 minutes by Nara Kotsu Bus from JR Hosono Station or Kintetsu Shin-Hosono Station.
