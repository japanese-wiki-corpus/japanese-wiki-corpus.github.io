Shimogamohigashi-dori Street (下鴨東通)

Shimogamohigashi-dori Street is a street running south-north through Sakyo Ward, Kyoto City. The street runs north from the west end of Kawai-bashi Bridge on the west shore of the Takano-gawa River (Kyoto City) to Uma-hashi Bridge. The street is located in a residential area.

Main Facilities on the Street
Kyoto Family Court: the east end of Aoi-bashi Bridge
Kyoto City Shimogamo Junior High School
Matsugasaki Water Treatment Plant
