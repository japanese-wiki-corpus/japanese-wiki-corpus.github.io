Manshuin-michi (曼殊院道)

Manshuin-michi is a street running east-west in Sakyo Ward in Kyoto City and runs eastward from Kawabata-dori Street to the gates of Manshu-in Temple.

Main Facilities along Manshuin-michi
Manshu-in Temple
Sagimori-jinja Shrine
Shisen-do Temple
Tanukidanisan-fudoin Temple
Hachidai-jinja Shrine at Ichijo-ji Temple sagari-matsu
Ichijoji Station on Eizan Electric Railway
Kyoto Municpal Shugakuin Junior High School
Kyoto Municpal Shugakuin Second Elementary School
