Itsutsuji-dori Street (五辻通)

Itsutsuji-dori Street is a street running east-west in Kyoto City. Itsutsuji-dori Street runs westward from a point on Omiya-dori Street to the point on Onmae-dori Street in front of the east gate of Kitano Tenmangu Shrine in Kamishichiken.

Main Facilities along Itsutsuji-dori Street
Kyoto Municipla Karaku Junior High School
Senbon-shakado Shakyamuni Hall (Daihoon-ji Temple)
Kamishichiken
Kitano Tenmangu Shrine
