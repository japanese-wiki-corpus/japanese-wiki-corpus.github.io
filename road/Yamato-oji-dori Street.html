Yamato-oji-dori Street (大和大路通)

The Yamato-oji-dori Street is a street running north-south in Kyoto City. It runs from Sanjo-dori Street in the north to Sennyuji-michi (a road south of Ikkyo Elementary School) in the south. It corresponds to the former Nara-kaido Road.

The section north of Shijo-dori Street is called Nawate-dori Street (Nawate--a bank of the Kamo-gawa River (the Yodo-gawa water system)).

Kennin-ji Temple

Hoko-ji Temple

Toyokuni/Hokoku jinja Shrine (Kyoto City)
