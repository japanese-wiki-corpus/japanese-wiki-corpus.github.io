Higashi Kuramaguchi-dori Street (東鞍馬口通)

Higashi Kuramaguchi-dori Street is a street running east-west in Kyoto City. The street runs from Kamihate-cho which is on a junction of three roads with Shirakawa-dori Street in the east to Shimogamohigashi-dori Street which is across the Tadekura-bashi Bridge of Takano-gawa River (Kyoto City) in the west. Kuramaguchi-dori Street is interrupted from Shimogamohigashi-dori Street to the east gate of Kamo Mioyaj-inja Shrine.
