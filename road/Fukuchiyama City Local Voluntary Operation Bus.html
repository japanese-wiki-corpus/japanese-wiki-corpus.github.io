Fukuchiyama City Local Voluntary Operation Bus (福知山市内自主運行バス)

The Fukuchiyama City Local Voluntary Operation Bus service is operated by Fukuchiyama City (Kyoto Prefecture) as a replacement for the discontinued regular bus route (No. 80 Route).

There are three routes for this bus service; Nakamutobe Bus, Anga Bus and Mitake Bus.

Summary

All three routes began their operations on October 1, 1994.

The operation of the bus is on weekdays and Saturday, while there is no operation on Sunday. There is no bus operation for new year holiday season, from December 30 to January 3.

The fare of this bus service is based on the travel distance, added to the base fare.

At bus stops in the suburbs or the center of the city where the Fukuchiyama City Local Voluntary Operation Bus is in competition with Kyoto Transport (Maizuru Management Office) and West JR Bus, you can only get off buses heading for Fukuchiyama and you can only get on buses departing from Fukuchiyama.

Nakamutobe Bus Route

The Nakamutobe Bus Board of Operation is managing the operation of the buses of this route.

Civic Hospital Front - Fukuchiyama Station - City Hall Front - Shinkai - Tano Yamada Community Center Front

Anga Bus Route

The Anga Bus Board of Operation is managing the operation of the buses of this route.

Civic Hospital Front - Fukuchiyama Station - Hazumaki

Mitake Bus Route

The Mitake Bus Board of Operation is managing the operation of the buses of this route,

City Hall Front - Fukuchiyama Station - Kamisasaki

Although the Zentan Bus Co. Ltd. used to operate a bus route coming from Hyogo Prefecture by going across the prefectural boarder and connecting to the Fukuchiyama City Local Voluntary Operation Bus route at Kamisasaki bus stop, the bus route was discontinued on September 30, 2008.
