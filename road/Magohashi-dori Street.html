Magohashi-dori Street (孫橋通)

Magohashi-dori Street is a street running east-west through the western part of Sakyo-Ward, Kyoto City. The street is one block north of Sanjo-dori Street and runs from Shintakakura-dori Street in the east to Kawabata-dori Street in the west. Sometimes the eastern extension in Furukawamachi-dori Street is called Magohashi-dori Street.

Also, sometimes the street to the east which runs from Furukawamachi-dori Street to Jingumichi road is called Sanjokitaura-dori Street.
