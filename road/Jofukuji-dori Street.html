Jofukuji-dori Street (浄福寺通)

Jofukuji-dori Street is a street running south-north through Kyoto City. This street runs south from Kenkun-jinja Shrine on Funaokaminami-dori Street, and turns at Rozanji-dori Street and Teranouchi-dori Street, heading south from in front of Jofuku-ji Temple to Takeyamachi-dori Street. Bojo-dori Street is located at the southern extension.

Main Facilities on the Street
Kenkun-jinja Shrine
Shonen-ji Temple (Neko-dera Temple): Teranouchi-dori Street agaru
Jofuku-ji Temple: Ichijo-dori Street agaru
Kyoto City Seishin Elementary School: Ninnaji kaido
Kyoto City Nijokita Elementary School: Sawaragicho-dori Street
