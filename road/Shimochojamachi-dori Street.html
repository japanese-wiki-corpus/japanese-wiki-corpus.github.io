Shimochojamachi-dori Street (下長者町通)

Shimochojamachi-dori Street is a street runing east-west street in Kamigyo Ward, Kyoto City. This street runs from Karasuma-dori Street in the east to Senbon-dori Street nishi-iru in the west.

The residential area has minimal traffic.

Main Facilities on the Street
Kyoto Gyoen (Kyoto Imperial Garden) Hamagurigomon Gate: Karasuma-dori Stret agaru
Goo-jinja Shrine
Kyoto Prefectural Government: from Shinmachi-dori Street to Nishinotoin-dori Street.
