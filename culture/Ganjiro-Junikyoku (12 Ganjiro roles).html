Ganjiro-Junikyoku (12 Ganjiro roles) (玩辞楼十二曲)

Ganjiro-Junikyoku refers to the specialty plays of Ganjiro NAKAMURA of Narikomaya selected by Ganjiro NAKAMURA the first.

Kawasho

Shigure no Kotatsu (The coverlet drenched with tears)

Fuinkiri

Akanezome

Koino Mizuumi (The Lake of Love)

Gobantaiheiki

Chikara TSUCHIYA (kabuki)

Wankyusuenomatsuyama

Tojuro no Koi (Tojuro's Love)

Kuruwa Bunsho (Love Letters from the Licensed Quarter)

Hikimado (skylight)

Katakiuchi Tuzureno Nishiki (Splendid revenge by the man who wore rag)
