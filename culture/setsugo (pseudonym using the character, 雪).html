setsugo (pseudonym using the character, 雪) (雪号)

Setsugo is a pseudonym after the retirement, using a Chinese character, '雪,' in shite-kata (main roles) of Kanze school of Noh. Only the head and the branch families are allowed to use the name, and in case of disciples using the name, it is limited to someone who has a successful career in style or is an honored master.
