Minoru UMEWAKA (梅若実)

Minoru UMEWAKA is the name used as an "inkyomei" (name after retirement) in the UMEWAKA family, shite-kata (main roles) of Kanze school of Noh. For details, refer to respective articles.

Minoru UMEWAKA, the 1st: inkyomei of Rokuro UMEWAKA, the 52nd.

Minoru UMEWAKA, the 2nd: inkyomei of Rokuro UMEWAKA (the 2nd son of the 1st), the 54th.
