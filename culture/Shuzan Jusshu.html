Shuzan Jusshu (秀山十種)

Shuzan Jusshu refers to the specialty plays of Kichiemon NAKAMURA of Harimaya (kabuki) selected by Kichiemon NAKAMURA the first. "Shuzan" is Kichiemon's haigo (the pen name of a haiku poet).
