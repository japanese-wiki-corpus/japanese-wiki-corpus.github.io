Fukujuen (福寿園)

Fukujuen CO., LTD is a tea company located in Yamashiro-cho, Kizugawa City, Kyoto Prefecture. It is known by the name of Iemon.

Summary
Company Name Fukujuen CO., LTD
Representatives
Chairman of board of directors Masafumi FUKUI
President of the company Masanori FUKUI
Headquarters 11 Higashi tsukuri michi, Kamikoma, Yamashiro-cho, Kizugawa City, Kyoto Prefecture

History

1790: Founded
1949: Incorporated as a company
1952: Opened first direct sales store in Kyoto station
2007: Opened Fukujuen Uji-kobo (interpretive center) in Uji City
2008: Opened Kyoto main shop in Kyoto City
