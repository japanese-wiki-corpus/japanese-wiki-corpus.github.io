Miyabi (雅)

Miyabi or Masa (in case of name) means sophisticated and graceful. Courtly and urban. And fine appearance.

Personal and other names

雅 is used for personal names relatively often and read 'Miyabi' for female names in Japanese reading and 'Masa' for male names in phonetic reading.

Trade names

雅 (cigarette) (Miyabi)

A brand name of cigarettes once sold.

Miyabi (rail car)

A nickname of Japanese style passenger car that was once used by Japanese National Railways.
