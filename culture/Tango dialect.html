Tango dialect (丹後弁)

Tango dialect is a kind of Japanese dialect that is spoken mainly in an area encompassing Miyazu City, Kyoto Prefecture and Kyotango City, Kyoto Prefecture. Its vocabulary and pronunciation is similar to Chugoku dialect.

Sentences tend to end in '-daaya', '-dade', '-waya' and '-wa.'

"Doude" (dode) means 'why?'
